Motors:
	Big Wheel (motor 1): right stick
	Arm (motor 0): left stick
	
Servos:
	Claw (servo 0): a to close, 
					b to open
					
	CR Servo (servo 1): dpad_up to go up, 
						dpad_down to go down, 
						left bumper to stop
	
	Bottom Servo (servo 2): dpad_left to go left position, 
							dpad_right to go to right position
